# How Upwork works

The mechanics that shape your earnings and ranking. Verify current numbers on
Upwork, fees and thresholds change.

## Fees

Upwork's service fee has historically tiered down as your lifetime billings with
a *given client* grow, a higher cut on the first chunk of earnings, dropping as
that relationship crosses higher lifetime totals. Check the current rate, and
remember it's per-client, so long relationships get cheaper.

## Job Success Score (JSS)

JSS blends public and private client feedback, long-term relationships, contract
outcomes (completed vs. cancelled), and your responsiveness. To protect it:

- Close inactive contracts: stale open ones drag the score.
- Never go silent on a contract; communicate before you step away.
- Ask for private feedback before the public review lands.
- Resolve problems before they escalate into disputes.

## Contract types

| Type | Best for | Main risk |
|------|----------|-----------|
| Fixed-price | Clear scope, defined deliverables | Scope creep |
| Hourly | Ongoing or fuzzy scope | Time-tracking scrutiny |
| Milestone | Large fixed projects | Protect with partial payments |

Fixed-price rule: never start work before the milestone is funded in escrow.
Hourly rule: use the tracker properly and write detailed work memos.

## Connects

- A small monthly allotment of free Connects, more with paid memberships.
- Each job costs a few Connects depending on complexity.
- Refunded if the job is cancelled or you're hired.
- Boosting spends extra Connects for visibility: only worth it with a strong
  profile.

## Account levels

| Level | Rough bar | Payoff |
|-------|-----------|--------|
| Rising Talent | New but promising | Badge visibility |
| Top Rated | ~90+ JSS, ~$1k+ earned, ~90 days | Better search placement, faster support |
| Top Rated Plus | Sustained excellence on larger contracts | Appears in the "Top Rated" hiring filter |
